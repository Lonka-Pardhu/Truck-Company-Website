On the road to success

The German Skiing Association (Deutscher Skiverband) can count on MAN as a reliable partner for commercial vehicles. As part of their partnership two MAN TGX, each with a semitrailer, will be made available to Germany’s national biathlon and cross-country skiing teams for use as waxing trucks. With the intelligent MAN HydroDrive® auxiliary drive system, the trucks can also cope with extreme weather conditions, which means that they can transport the athletes' kit wherever they need it.
